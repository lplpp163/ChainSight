⚙ AI-Enabled Solution

Lightweight factory digitalization for semiconductor SME suppliers — 3 AI modules:

Pulse
5G IoT → real-time OEE, yield on client Portal. AI auto-classifies machine status.
Predict
ML predicts failure 7-14 days ahead, material depletion, true delivery dates.
Comply
Auto-maps to RBA/CSRD/OSH Act. One-click audit reports.

Why AI is essential: Transforms raw IoT signals into actionable predictions — decisions that required engineers in short supply (MY needs 60K; produces 5K/yr).

★ Innovation
  • Position: Not another ERP — fills the real-time gap between ERP (lagging) and factory floor (live) via 5G IoT. Complements SAP, not competes.
  • Biz Model: "Fabs pay, SMEs deploy free." Intel/ASE pay Portal subscription → subsidize SME IoT install → triple-win.
  • Network Effect: More suppliers → more visibility → fabs pay more → flywheel that competitors without telecom infra cannot replicate.
  • Cross-border: Taiwan HQ monitors MY factories via CHT submarine cables + SD-WAN — 24/7 resilient.
